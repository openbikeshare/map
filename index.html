Openbike.nl

Looking for a shared bike?
Confused by all those different options?

Openbike.nl is the solution by giving an overview of all available bikes. Openbike is a free service and is open-source.

Openbike.nl uses the open standard GBFS+ which is supported by:

  • 12 bike companies in The Netherlands
  • The ministry of Infrastructure
  • The cities of Amsterdam, Rotterdam, Utrecht, The Hague and Eindhoven
  • CROW Fietsberaad

Get involved

Openbike.nl and the open standard GBFS+ is not finished. Please help to make this project awesome. Together we can develop Open Bike Sharing tools. We strive for a transparent, decentralized solution. Below you find the communication channels we use at the moment and some links. Feel free to join, we love to hear from you!
